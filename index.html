Welcome to Ping Pong! a game that will take a number from a user and return a range of numbers from 1 to the chosen number, with the following exceptions:

  • Numbers divisible by 3 are replaced with "ping"
  • Numbers divisible by 5 are replaced with "pong"
  • Numbers divisible by 15 are replaced with "pingpong"
  • A user will be able to enter a new number and see the NEW results over and over again.
